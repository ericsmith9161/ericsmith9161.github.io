POLISEE

ROLE: BACKEND LEAD

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Polisee is a single-page political information/contacting app where users can get information of politicians in fields of their interest at various governing levels, follow those politicians to stay informed and receive reminders about upcoming elections.

Enter your address, interests and desired level of government to see your representatives


View basic information from the results page, or click on the representative for additional information


Including voting records, social media links, re-election years



as well as recent news stories involving them


Create an account to follow representatives

You can also update your interests so they're available each time you hop on Polisee


As well as set e-mail reminders that relay representative contact information
